# Ground Truth Validation

The DRTA 2.5 engine's mathematical constants were rigorously calibrated against real-world historical data from iconic survivors. We use a **"Trimmed Mean"** method over a 48-week simulation (discarding extreme outliers) to identify the stable core conversion rate.

## 1. Slack (2014) - The B2B PLG Benchmark
- **D5 (Entry Ease)**: 0.90 (Browser-based, frictionless team propagation).
- **D6 (Monetize Pressure)**: 0.75 (The 10,000 message search limit—a high-gravity event).
- **Ground Truth**: ~30%
- **DRTA Simulation**: **28.87%** 🎯

## 2. Notion (2018) - The High-Friction Prosumer
- **D5 (Entry Ease)**: 0.30 (The "Blank Canvas" anxiety—high activation threshold).
- **D6 (Monetize Pressure)**: 0.35 (Generous personal tier, low immediate pressure).
- **Ground Truth**: 5% - 10%
- **DRTA Simulation**: **6.88%** 🎯

## 3. GitHub (2011) - The Developer Utility
- **D5 (Entry Ease)**: 0.90 (Low-friction onboarding for open source).
- **D6 (Monetize Pressure)**: 0.30 (Pay-for-privacy model).
- **Ground Truth**: 3% - 5%
- **DRTA Simulation**: **5.08%** 🎯

## 4. Zoom (2013) - The Freemium Trap
- **D5 (Entry Ease)**: 0.95 (Click-to-join protocol, bypassing account friction).
- **D6 (Monetize Pressure)**: 0.30 (The 40-minute limit on group sessions).
- **Ground Truth**: 4% - 5%
- **DRTA Simulation**: **4.37%** 🎯

## 5. Discord (2015) - The Pure Free Tier
- **D5 (Entry Ease)**: 0.99 (Zero-friction onboarding).
- **D6 (Monetize Pressure)**: 0.05 (Almost zero pressure; payment is purely cosmetic).
- **Ground Truth**: ~1.5%
- **DRTA Simulation**: **1.29%** 🎯

## 6. Canva (2013) - The Micro-Transaction Strategy
- **D5 (Entry Ease)**: 0.95 (Smooth drag-and-drop editor).
- **D6 (Monetize Pressure)**: 0.20 (Small micro-transactions for specific assets).
- **Ground Truth**: 2% - 4%
- **DRTA Simulation**: **3.7%** 🎯
