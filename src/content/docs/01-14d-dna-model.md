# The 14D Commercial DNA Model

LemeoneLab treats startups not as business plans, but as physical entities defined by a 14-dimensional vector. Every calculation in the sandbox leverages all 14 dimensions simultaneously to simulate the complex interference patterns of a real market.

## Core Dimensions (D1-D4): The Value Engine
These dimensions dictate the raw **Resonance** (how much people want the product).
- **D1 (Performance)**: Speed, latency, and raw computational reliability.
- **D2 (Depth)**: Feature completeness and power-user logic density.
- **D3 (Interaction)**: UX fluidity and the aesthetic "haptic" feedback.
- **D4 (Stability)**: Security, uptime, and structural trust.

## The Funnel Gates (D5-D6): The Business Model
These dimensions are decoupled to simulate the "Energy Gap" between usage and revenue.
- **D5 (Entry Ease)**: The "Activation Threshold." 1.0 represents frictionless access; 0.0 represents heavy enterprise sales hurdles.
- **D6 (Monetize Pressure)**: The "Paywall Gravity." Determines the force pushing users from free to paid tiers.

## Market Dynamics (D7-D9)
- **D7 (Unique Value)**: Differentiation against the existing market baseline gravity.
- **D8 (Social Virality)**: The inherent K-factor and peer-to-peer transmission coefficient.
- **D9 (Consistency)**: Execution delivery matching marketing promises.

## Strategic Future (D10-D13)
- **D10 (Ecosystem)**: Platform effects, APIs, and developer neural networks.
- **D11 (Barriers)**: Moats against cloning (data gravity and network effects).
- **D12 (Global Appeal)**: Cross-cultural / cross-language viability and scale.
- **D13 (Growth Curve)**: The shape of the expected scaling function over time.

## Go-To-Market (D14)
- **D14 (Awareness)**: The initial distribution blast on Day 1. Driven by PR, founder reputation, or algorithmic discovery.

## Reality Anchoring System (Deep Research)
LemeoneLab does not operate in a vacuum.

While extracting your product's DNA, our **News Parser Service** scans the live web for industry reports, competitor maneuvers, and macro-economic shifts. This data is injected into the sandbox as "Environmental Perturbations" and "Macro-Gravitational Constants."

**Operational Warning**: If you are simulating an AI video tool and OpenAI drops a new model like Sora, the market gravity in your sandbox will shift instantly, potentially causing a "Survival Collapse" for your project.
